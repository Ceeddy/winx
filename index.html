Questions? Answers.

Everything you need to know before getting started with Winx.

What exactly does Winx build?
+
We build custom AI chatbots and automation workflows tailored to your business — chatbots for your website or WhatsApp, automated lead follow-ups, content scheduling, and more.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
+
Not at all! We handle everything from setup to deployment. You just tell us about your business and we take care of the rest.
How long does it take to go live?
+
Most projects go live within 3–7 days. Simple chatbots can be ready in as little as 48 hours. Enterprise solutions may take 2–3 weeks.
Is my business data safe?
+
Absolutely. Your data is used only to train your chatbot and is never shared with third parties. We can sign an NDA before any work begins.
What if I want changes after launch?
+
All plans include ongoing support. We update your chatbot whenever your business information changes and optimize performance over time.
